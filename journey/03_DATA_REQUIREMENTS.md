### Segment definitions (set once here, reused by BQ-05/06/09 — not reinvented per mart)
- **Income band**: LOW (< 25th percentile of `AMT_INCOME_TOTAL` at seed time), MEDIUM (25th–75th),
  HIGH (> 75th) — percentile computed once at Silver build time on the seeded Home Credit
  population and frozen (not recomputed per run, so bands don't drift under re-runs).
- **Employment type**: pass through Home Credit's `NAME_INCOME_TYPE` categories directly (no
  re-bucketing needed — the source categories are already business-meaningful).
- **Dormancy window (X)**: **90 days** with no transaction across ANY of the customer's linked
  accounts (xwalk-resolved) — chosen to match the "active" definition used in BQ-05, so the two
  BQs don't silently use different activity windows.
- **"Healthy" deposit balance (BQ-06)**: account balance in the top 50th percentile of the seeded
  Berka/OBP deposit population, computed the same way as the income band (frozen at Silver build).

## Non-functional requirements
- **Freshness**: batch, run-on-demand (D-02) — no real-time SLA in v1; BQ-10 surfaces staleness
  rather than the pipeline enforcing a freshness contract.
- **Retention**: Landing = short TTL (transient arrival zone, D-15 — e.g. 7 days, matches the kind
  of TTL CIL used for its own landing zone, ADR-007 precedent); Bronze = permanent, append-only
  (D-05); Silver/Gold = permanent, rebuildable from Bronze at any time (Bronze is the source of
  truth for replay). Rationale: replay/audit (Bronze) vs re-ingest-safety window (Landing).
- **PII/sensitive fields**: `birth_number` (Berka), account numbers (all sources), card numbers
  (PaySim/MSSQL), `isFraud` label, balances/amounts. Full classification + handling in
  `journey/09_SECURITY_AND_ACCESS.md` §2; masking mechanics in `journey/06_DQ_PLAN.md`.
